Our Treatments

Flap Surgery


Flap surgery is a periodontal treatment used to treat advanced gum disease. During this procedure, the dentist lifts the gums away from the teeth to access and remove plaque and tartar buildup. This allows for thorough cleaning of the roots and helps reduce inflammation. The gums are then repositioned and sutured back into place, promoting healing and restoring gum health.
